# APAC Client Success Operating Rhythm 2026

**Status:** Final | **Version:** 1.0 | **Date:** 29 January 2026

---

## Purpose

This document outlines the 2026 Client Success operating rhythm for APAC. It serves as a reference for CSEs, CAMs, and leadership to understand key actions, events, and milestones throughout the year. The goal is to enable teams to plan ahead, execute on time, and align activities with overarching business objectives.

---

## Strategic Context

### 2026 Business Imperatives

Based on APAC Compass 2026, the region faces critical challenges that inform our operating rhythm:

| Metric | Value | Implication |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| **Confirmed Attrition (FY25-28)** | $2.72M | Phased over 3 years; largest hit ($1.12M Sunrise) in FY2028 |
| **CSE Weighted Pipeline** | $8.36M | At 35% conversion = $2.93M realistic new revenue |
| **Revenue Concentration** | 4 clients = ~80% maintenance | SA Health alone = 21% of maintenance revenue |
| **NPS Volatility** | SA Health: 6.1 avg (range 1-9) | Stakeholder-specific strategies required |

### Strategic Priorities

1. **Protect run-rate revenue** through proactive client engagement and satisfaction improvements
2. **Convert pipeline** by targeting improving accounts with higher probability
3. **Address product perception** through clear roadmap communication
4. **Deepen client relationships** via segmentation-driven engagement

## Ongoing Activities: Segmentation Actions

### Year-Round Engagement Cadence

| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Activity** | Segmentation Actions |
| **Period** | January - December 2026 |
| **Participants** | AVP CS, CSEs, CAMs |
| **Objective** | Execute segmentation-driven engagement activities consistently throughout the year |

### Segmentation Framework

| Segment | Definition | CS Strategy | Sales Strategy |
|---------|------------|-------------|----------------|
| **Maintain** | Low satisfaction, low spend | Increase satisfaction through intensive support and communication | Prevent attrition; re-establish trust before sales conversations |
| **Leverage** | Satisfied, low spend | Maintain satisfaction; identify growth opportunities | Increase annual spend through targeted proposals |
| **Nurture** | High spend, low satisfaction | Targeted satisfaction improvement | Protect revenue; delay expansion until satisfaction improves |
| **Collaborate** | Satisfied, moderate spend | Deepen strategic partnership | Expand products and services |
| **Sleeping Giants** | High spend, moderate satisfaction | Focused satisfaction improvement | Maintain contract value; careful expansion |
| **Giants** | High satisfaction, high spend | Deepen executive alignment | Maximise value and contract term |

### Activity Register Requirements

The Client Segmentation Activity Register defines minimum engagement requirements per segment:

| Activity | Maintain | Leverage | Nurture | Collaborate | Sleeping Giant | Giant |
|----------|----------|----------|---------|-------------|----------------|-------|
| Partnership Meetings | Monthly | Quarterly | Monthly | Quarterly | Monthly | Quarterly |
| Service Review | Monthly | Quarterly | Monthly | Quarterly | Monthly | Quarterly |
| Health Check | Quarterly | Bi-annual | Quarterly | Bi-annual | Quarterly | Bi-annual |
| QBR | Quarterly | Quarterly | Quarterly | Quarterly | Quarterly | Quarterly |
| Executive Engagement | As needed | Bi-annual | Quarterly | Bi-annual | Quarterly | Quarterly |

### Satisfaction Action Plan Requirements

Clients in **Maintain**, **Nurture**, and **Sleeping Giant** segments require active Satisfaction Action Plans:

**Template Structure:**
1. Action description
2. Altera business function responsible
3. People accountable
4. Timeframe
5. Success criteria
6. Measurement method
7. Review dates and progress updates

**Review Cadence:** Monthly progress updates; Quarterly formal reviews

## Risk Management

### Known Risk Areas (2026)

Based on APAC Compass analysis:

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| **SA Health volatility** | $6.8M at risk (NPS 6.1 avg, range 1-9) | Stakeholder-specific strategies; identify and expand with satisfied departments |
| **Pipeline conversion** | At 35% conversion, barely covers attrition | Focus on improving accounts with higher probability; WA Health, GHA |
| **Product perception** | "Legacy/outdated" feedback affecting renewals | Clear roadmap communication; feature demonstrations |
| **Revenue concentration** | 4 clients = ~80% maintenance revenue | Diversification through new logos; protect existing relationships |

### Early Warning Indicators

Monitor these metrics monthly:

- Client health score changes (>10% decline)
- Support backlog by segment
- NPS response rates (target >40%)
- Pipeline stage movement
- Engagement compliance (segmentation activities)

## Appendix A: NPS Process Checklist

### Pre-Survey (T-2 weeks)

- [ ] Validate recipient lists in Microsoft
- [ ] Identify unsubscribed contacts
- [ ] CACs contact unsubscribed clients for resubscription
- [ ] Upload validated list to HubSpot
- [ ] Confirm survey configuration

### During Survey (2 weeks)

- [ ] Monitor delivery rates in HubSpot
- [ ] Pull non-responder list (Day 5)
- [ ] Send reminder email (Day 6)
- [ ] Pull final non-responder list (Day 12)
- [ ] Manual follow-up for strategic accounts
- [ ] Close survey (Day 14)

### Post-Survey (4 weeks)

- [ ] Pull raw data from HubSpot
- [ ] Conduct analysis workshop (Week 1)
- [ ] Develop BU talking points (Week 2)
- [ ] EVP approval (Week 3)
- [ ] Send client letters (Week 4)
