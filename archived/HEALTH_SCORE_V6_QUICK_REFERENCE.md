## Quick Diagnostics

### If Score Drops...

**Check Engagement First** (30 pts)
- Has NPS declined? (Check latest survey)
- Missing QBRs? (Check compliance %)

**Then Financial** (40 pts)
- Revenue trending down? (Check YoY growth)
- AR aging? (Check payment patterns)
- Contract at risk? (Check renewal date/risk)

**Then Operational** (20 pts)
- Actions piling up? (Check open count)
- Support issues? (Check ticket volume)

**Finally Strategic** (10 pts)
- Expansion stalled? (Check opportunity pipeline)

## Best Practices

### For CSEs
1. Review category breakdown, not just total score
2. Focus on lowest-scoring category
3. Update expansion opportunities regularly
4. Keep actions current (completion or closure)
5. Monitor revenue trends monthly

### For Managers
1. Use v6.0 for quarterly business reviews
2. Compare category scores across portfolio
3. Identify systemic issues (e.g., all low support)
4. Prioritise renewal risk clients
5. Align resources to category needs

### For Executives
1. Financial health (40%) is primary indicator
2. Category trends show organisational health
3. Expansion scores indicate growth potential
4. Use for capacity planning and resource allocation
